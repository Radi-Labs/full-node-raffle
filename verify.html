This is the same calculation as verify.py and the draw script, reimplemented in client-side JavaScript so it depends on nothing you have to trust. Single block: int(blockhash, 16) mod N. Several blocks: int(SHA-256(concatenated raw hash bytes)) mod N. Entries are stripped, de-duplicated, and sorted before hashing.
